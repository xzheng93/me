Xiaoping Zheng is currently a PostDoct in the Department of Sport Science and Physical Education at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, working under the supervision of Prof. Yijian Yang.
   He received his Ph.D. from the University of Groningen / University Medical Center Groningen, where he was guided by Prof. Claudine Lamoth, Prof. Michiel Reneman and Prof. Bert Otten.
   His research interests encompass gait and physical activity intensity analysis, machine learning, explainable artificial intelligence, wearable sensors, and time series analysis.
